Life expectancy versus Health Expenditure

Source: OECD: Life expectancy, Health expenditure

This website allows you to see whether their might be a correlation between the healt expenditure of a country and the life expectancy at birth of its population.

  • US dollars/capita
  • % of GDP

Health Expenditure Members European Union in US dollars/capita(2015)

The map above gives information about the total health expenditure of every country in the European Union. Hover over the map to see how much dollar or what (rounded) percentage of the Gross domestic product (GDP) a specific country spends on its healtcare. The barchart will also show more information about a specific country when you hover over that country in the map. Note that non-EU countries are left out.

Life Expactancy at Birth (Male, Female and Combined) (2015)

The bar chart above shows the life expectancy at birth in years for a specific country. The life expectancy is shown for the male and the female population, as well as the life expectancy of the total population. Hover over each bar to see the exact age which is represented by each bar seperately.
